## Core Principles
- **File before you're famous.** Trademark protection is first-to-file in most countries (not first-to-use like the US). If someone in China files "Vectorial Data" before you, they own it there.
- **A domain is not a trademark.** Owning vectorialdata.com does not give you trademark rights. They're separate legal regimes.
- **Content is automatically copyrighted.** Your research, analysis, and written content are protected by copyright from the moment of creation. But registration strengthens enforcement.
- **Data is not copyrightable.** Stock prices, P/E ratios, and financial data are facts. Your analysis and presentation ARE copyrightable.
- **Defensive registration > reactive litigation.** Filing trademarks costs hundreds. Opposing a squatter costs tens of thousands.
- **Monitor your brand.** If someone starts "Vectorial Data México" or "VectorialPicks," you need to know fast.

## Trademark Strategy

### Phase 1: US Filing (NOW)
- **File with USPTO** in Classes:
  - **Class 36:** Financial analysis; providing financial information via a website
  - **Class 41:** Educational services in the field of finance and investing
- **Filing basis:** Use in commerce (1(a)) if already selling, or Intent to Use (1(b))
- **Cost:** ~$250-350 per class (TEAS Plus filing)
- **Timeline:** ~12-18 months to registration

### Phase 2: International via Madrid Protocol (SOON)
- Use US registration as basis for international filing
- **Priority countries:** Mexico, Brazil, EU (EUIPO covers all 27 countries), UK, India, Australia, Singapore
- **Cost:** ~$600-1,000 per jurisdiction designation
- **Madrid Protocol advantage:** Single application, single fee, covers multiple countries

### Phase 3: Individual Filings (WHERE NEEDED)
- Countries not in Madrid Protocol or where direct filing is stronger
- **China** — File defensively even if not operating there (trademark squatting is epidemic)
- **UAE, Saudi Arabia** — If MENA expansion planned

### Defensive Domain Strategy
Register and park:
- vectorialdata.com ✓ (primary)
- vectorialdata.net, .org, .io (defensive)
- vectorial-data.com (hyphenated variant)
- Country-specific: vectorialdata.com.mx, vectorialdata.com.br
- Common misspellings
